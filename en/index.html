About us

Connecting people.
Helping in practice.

We are a non-profit organization from Nuremberg bringing together people from different countries and cultures. We're driven by the vision of a united, peaceful and diverse Europe — and the conviction that solidarity overcomes borders.

Since 2022 we have organized humanitarian aid, medical equipment and cultural encounters. We work transparently, on a volunteer basis, and document every project in a traceable way.
