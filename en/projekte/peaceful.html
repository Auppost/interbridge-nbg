Piece of Peaceful Sky

28 children from FC "Atlet" Kyiv — three full U12 teams — spent a week in Nuremberg sleeping, training and playing without air raid sirens. To top it off, the boys took gold at the international intercultural football festival in Leipzig — winning the final 2:1 against hosts BSG Chemie Leipzig.
